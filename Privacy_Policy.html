Privacy Policy

Personal data

Extensions have never collected and will not collect any personal data, browsing history etc. Also no data related to accessing external resources is collected.

In the future my extensions may collect data regarding browser (version), platform, display settings. Data collected would always be anonymous and require the the user’s consent. This data may be used to improve the services the extensions offer. At the time of writing (November 6, 2020) no extensions have been published capable of collecting this data.

Third party services

UTwente Dark uses Chromium Storage Sync API for storing the user’s settings.

UTwente Dark loads styles from GitHub's servers. You agree to GitHub's Privacy Policy when using the UTDark extension.
